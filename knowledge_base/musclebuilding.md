# SECTION: Muscle Building Strategy

## Summary
Muscle growth requires resistance training, proper nutrition, and recovery.

## Key Principles
- progressive overload
- sufficient protein intake
- adequate rest

## Example Routine
- train each muscle group 2 times per week

## Nutrition
- protein intake: 1.6–2.2g per kg body weight

## Risks
- overtraining
- poor nutrition

## Recommendations
- follow structured workout plan
- track progress
