# Construction Planning & Material Cost Baselines
**Domain:** Construction
**Last Updated:** Q2 2026

## Overview
This document provides benchmark pricing, variance thresholds, and timeline estimates for standard residential and commercial construction projects. Agents should use these figures to calculate budgets, apply contingencies, and schedule project phases.

## Standard Material Costs (USD)

### Foundation & Concrete
*   **Ready-Mix Concrete:** $130 - $150 per cubic yard. (High variance: Can spike to $170 in urban areas).
*   **Rebar (Steel):** $0.80 - $1.20 per pound.
*   **Concrete Blocks:** $1.50 - $2.50 per block.

### Framing & Lumber
*   **Dimensional Lumber (2x4):** $3.50 - $5.00 per piece. (Highly volatile: apply a 15% risk buffer).
*   **Plywood (4x8 sheet):** $25.00 - $45.00 depending on thickness and grade.
*   **Structural Steel:** $2,500 - $3,200 per ton fabricated.

### Roofing & Exterior
*   **Asphalt Shingles:** $90 - $120 per square (100 sq ft).
*   **Metal Roofing:** $350 - $500 per square.
*   **Vinyl Siding:** $4.00 - $7.00 per square foot installed.
*   **Brick Veneer:** $9.00 - $15.00 per square foot installed.

### Interior Finishes
*   **Drywall (1/2 inch):** $12.00 - $18.00 per panel.
*   **Standard Flooring (LVP/Laminate):** $3.00 - $8.00 per square foot.
*   **Paint:** $30 - $55 per gallon.

## Labor Rates (Average Hourly)
*   **General Laborer:** $20 - $35/hr
*   **Electrician:** $65 - $110/hr
*   **Plumber:** $70 - $120/hr
*   **Carpenter:** $45 - $80/hr
*   **Project Manager:** $55 - $95/hr

## Standard Timeline Phases (Residential Build)
1.  **Site Prep & Foundation:** 2 - 4 weeks
2.  **Framing:** 3 - 6 weeks
3.  **MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) Rough-in:** 2 - 4 weeks
4.  **Insulation & Drywall:** 2 - 3 weeks
5.  **Interior Finishes:** 4 - 8 weeks
6.  **Final Fixtures & Walkthrough:** 1 - 2 weeks

## Risk & Variance Mitigation Rules
*   **Budget Contingency:** Always apply a mandatory 10% contingency for standard builds and a 15-20% contingency for custom or high-risk terrain builds.
*   **Weather Delays:** Add 2 weeks of buffer time for builds occurring during winter or rainy seasons.
*   **Supply Chain Buffer:** For specialized materials (e.g., custom windows, structural steel), add a 30-day lead time warning to the schedule.
