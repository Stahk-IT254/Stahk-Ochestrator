# SECTION: Income Growth and Diversification

## Summary
Increasing income is a key strategy for accelerating savings and achieving financial goals faster.

## Types of Income
- Active income: salary, wages
- Passive income: investments, rental income
- Side income: freelancing, small business

## Strategies to Increase Income
- Acquire new skills
- Start side businesses
- Invest in income-generating assets

## Example
If monthly income increases from KES 50,000 to KES 70,000:
- Savings capacity increases significantly without reducing lifestyle

## Risks
- unstable side income
- burnout from multiple income streams

## Recommendations
- diversify income sources gradually
- prioritize stable income streams
