# SECTION: Fitness Goal Planning

## Summary
Fitness goals require structured planning, consistency, and measurable progress.

## Types of Goals
- Weight loss
- Muscle gain
- Endurance improvement

## Goal Setting Principles
- Specific
- Measurable
- Achievable
- Time-bound

## Example
Lose 5kg in 3 months

## Key Components
- exercise plan
- diet plan
- rest and recovery

## Challenges
- lack of consistency
- unrealistic expectations
- poor tracking
