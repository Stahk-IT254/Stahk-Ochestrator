# SECTION: Financial Goal Optimization

## Summary
Optimizing financial goals involves balancing savings, spending, and investment.

## Optimization Strategies
- increase savings rate gradually
- reduce non-essential expenses
- reinvest returns

## Scenario Example
Goal: Save KES 200,000
Options:
- Save KES 10,000/month → 20 months
- Save KES 20,000/month → 10 months

## Trade-Offs
- faster savings reduces lifestyle flexibility
- slower savings increases financial comfort

## Advanced Strategy
- combine saving + investing for faster growth

## Recommendations
- review goals monthly
- adjust based on income changes
