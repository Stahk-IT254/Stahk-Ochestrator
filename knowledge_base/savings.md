# SECTION: Savings Planning Strategy

## Summary
A savings plan defines how much to save, how often, and for how long to reach a financial goal.

## Steps to Build a Savings Plan
1. Define target amount
2. Define timeline
3. Calculate monthly savings needed

## Example
Goal: KES 120,000 in 12 months  
Monthly savings required: KES 10,000

## Factors Affecting Savings
- income stability
- inflation
- emergency expenses

## Strategies
- automate savings
- increase income streams
- reduce expenses

## Trade-Offs
- faster savings → less spending
- slower savings → more flexibility

## Risks
- missing monthly contributions
- unexpected financial obligations
