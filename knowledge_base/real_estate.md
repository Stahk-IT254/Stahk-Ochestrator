# Real Estate Valuation Guidelines

1. **Location Factor**: Properties within 1 mile of a transit hub have a 15% premium.
2. **Construction Quality**: Standard homes cost $150 per sqft to build, luxury homes cost $250 per sqft.
3. **Depreciation**: Commercial properties depreciate over 39 years, residential over 27.5 years.
4. **Market Trends**: In 2026, suburban properties are seeing a 5% YoY increase in value.
