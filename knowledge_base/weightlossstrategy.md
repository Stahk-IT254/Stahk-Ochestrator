# SECTION: Weight Loss Strategy

## Summary
Weight loss is achieved through a calorie deficit, consistent exercise, and proper diet.

## Core Principle
- Burn more calories than consumed

## Methods
- reduce calorie intake
- increase physical activity
- combine both approaches

## Example
Daily calorie deficit of 500 kcal leads to gradual weight loss.

## Timeline
- safe weight loss: 0.5–1 kg per week

## Risks
- extreme dieting
- muscle loss

## Recommendations
- maintain moderate calorie deficit
- combine cardio and strength training
