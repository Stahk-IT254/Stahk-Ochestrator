# SECTION: Fitness Risks and Optimization

## Summary
Fitness progress depends on balancing intensity, recovery, and nutrition.

## Common Risks
- injury from improper technique
- burnout from overtraining
- poor diet affecting results

## Optimization Strategies
- progressive overload
- proper rest
- balanced nutrition

## Trade-Offs
- faster results increase injury risk
- slower progress is more sustainable

## Recommendations
- track workouts
- monitor progress weekly
- adjust intensity gradually
