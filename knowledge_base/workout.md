# SECTION: Workout Structure

## Summary
An effective workout plan includes strength training, cardio, and recovery.

## Weekly Plan Example
- 3 days strength training
- 2 days cardio
- 2 days rest

## Strength Training
- improves muscle mass
- increases metabolism

## Cardio
- burns calories
- improves heart health

## Recovery
- prevents injury
- improves performance

## Risks
- overtraining
- improper form
