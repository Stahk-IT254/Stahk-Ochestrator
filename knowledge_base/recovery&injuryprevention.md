# SECTION: Recovery and Injury Prevention

## Summary
Recovery is essential for long-term fitness progress and injury prevention.

## Recovery Methods
- rest days
- sleep (7–9 hours)
- stretching

## Injury Causes
- improper form
- overtraining
- lack of warm-up

## Prevention Strategies
- proper technique
- gradual progression
- adequate rest

## Risks
- ignoring pain signals
- excessive training intensity

## Recommendations
- listen to your body
- prioritize recovery
