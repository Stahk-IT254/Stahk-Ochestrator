# SECTION: Investment Fundamentals

## Summary
Investing allows money to grow over time and helps beat inflation.

## Types of Investments
- Savings accounts (low risk)
- Government bonds (moderate risk)
- Stocks (higher risk)
- Real estate

## Risk vs Return
- Higher returns come with higher risk
- Lower risk investments provide stability

## Time Horizon
- Short-term → low-risk investments
- Long-term → higher-risk investments acceptable

## Example
Investing KES 10,000 monthly at 8% annual return grows significantly over time.

## Risks
- market fluctuations
- poor investment choices

## Recommendations
- diversify investments
- avoid high-risk investments without knowledge
