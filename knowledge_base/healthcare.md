# Healthcare Compliance Standards

1. **Patient Data Privacy (HIPAA)**: All patient records must be encrypted at rest and in transit. Access must be logged.
2. **Telehealth**: Virtual consultations must be conducted over a certified, end-to-end encrypted platform.
3. **Billing Codes**: Use ICD-10 for diagnosis codes and CPT for procedure codes.
4. **Prescription Rules**: Schedule II drugs cannot be refilled without a new physical or verified electronic prescription.
