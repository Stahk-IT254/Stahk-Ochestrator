# Financial Auditing & Taxes

1. **Corporate Tax Rate**: The standard corporate tax rate is 21% of net income.
2. **Expense Deductibility**: Business meals are only 50% deductible.
3. **Audit Triggers**: Variances over 15% from the previous fiscal year in any major category will trigger an automatic internal review.
4. **Escrow Rules**: Funds held in escrow cannot be commingled with operating accounts.
