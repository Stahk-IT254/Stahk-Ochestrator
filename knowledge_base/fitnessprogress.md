# SECTION: Fitness Progress Tracking

## Summary
Tracking progress ensures consistency and helps adjust fitness plans.

## Metrics to Track
- body weight
- body measurements
- strength levels
- endurance

## Methods
- weekly weigh-ins
- workout logs
- progress photos

## Importance
- identifies plateaus
- helps adjust strategy

## Risks
- relying on inaccurate data
- inconsistent tracking

## Recommendations
- track progress weekly
- adjust plans based on results
