# SECTION: Financial Risks and Optimization

## Summary
Financial planning must account for risks and uncertainties to ensure stability.

## Common Risks
- loss of income
- medical emergencies
- inflation reducing purchasing power

## Emergency Fund
- recommended: 3–6 months of expenses

## Inflation Impact
- reduces value of saved money over time

## Optimization Techniques
- diversify income
- invest excess savings
- review financial plan regularly

## Trade-Offs
- saving more reduces current lifestyle
- spending more delays goals

## Recommendations
- maintain emergency fund
- track progress monthly
- adjust plan based on income changes
